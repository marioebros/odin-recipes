Lasagna Flatbread

Lasagna

Description

A simple lasagna pizza recipe to mix up the mundane. In less than an hour, you'll have be transported to the villas of Tuscany.

Ingredients

    15 ounces of Ricotta cheese 8 ounce shredded Mozzarella cheese 3 ounce Parmesan cheese 1 egg 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning 1 pound sausage 26 ounce jar - Marinara sauce 6 flatbreads

Steps

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Combine ricotta cheese, half of the Mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning in a bowl. Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink, for 5 to 10 minutes and proceed to drain. Stir in the Marinara suace. Spread the cheese mixture evenly on each flatbread; then cover with sausage mixture. Top off with the remaining mozzarella cheese. Place in preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbling, usually within 10 to 15 minutes.
