Real Poutine

Real Poutine

Description

A tried and true Canadian Classic! A smorgasbord of gravy, fries, and cheese curds.

Ingredients

    1 quart of vegetable oil for frying 10.25 ounce can of beef gravy 5 medium potatos, cut into fries 2 cups of cheese curds

Steps

    Heat your oil in a deep fryer of deep skillet to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C). While your oil is heating, you can begin to heat up your gravy. Place fries into the hot oil, and cook until light brown for about 5 minutes. Make fries in batches if necessary to allow room to move in the oil. Remove and place on paper towl lined plate to allow for draining. Place the fries on a serving platter and sprinkle cheese curds generously. Ladle the gravy over the fries and cheese and serve immediately.
