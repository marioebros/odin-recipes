Basic California Roll

Description

A classic take on a Basic California Roll. Easy to follow steps for even a complete beginner!

Ingredients

    Half of one cucumber 4 ounces crab sticks, finely chopped 4 tablespoons lime juice 4 sheets of nori 2 cups of cooked sushi rice 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds 1 avocado, sliced 4 teaspoons tobiko (flying fish roe)

Steps

    Slice the ends off of the cucumber, then cut into quarters lengthwise. Thinly slice each quarter lengthwise to make spears. Stir chopped crab sticks and lime juice together in a small bowl. Lay 1 sheet of nori on a bamboo mat. Pat a thin, even layer of rice over the nori with wet hands, leaving an edge bare at the bottom. Sprinkle sesame seeds over rice. Proceed to flip over. Add 1 tablespoon of crab mixture in a line across the sheet about 1/3 of the way from the top. Layer with 2 cucumber spears and 3 slices of avocado. Roll the sheet tightly into a cylinder, starting from the top. Moisten the uncovered edge with water and finish rolling once you reach the bottom. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Slice each roll into 3/4-inch pieces. Garnish each piece with 1/8 teaspoon tobiko.
